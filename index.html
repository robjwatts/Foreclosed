Foreclosed Studios

Midland Park, NJ. Est. 2017.

Welcome to Foreclosed Studios, an independent recording space in Northern New Jersey. Let's create something together.

Foreclosed Studios offers a unique recording space for your next musical project. With professional quality mixing at affordable rates, the apartment-turned-studio will be an artistic experience like no other. If you'd like to learn how to play music for yourself, or for someone special, lessons from an experienced teacher are also available. Contact the studio and say hello.
