About

What began as a personal passion has become of the most unique and inspiring recording spaces in the NYC metro area. Founder Steve Hara has been writing and recording music in a small apartment in Midland Park since 2014. As word spread, artists from New York, New Jersey, and beyond have come to the space to create and collaborate under the tutelage of Hara’s recording and mixing experience. As of 2017, Foreclosed Studios is open for business. All who love music are welcome.

If you are looking to learn the art of audio mixing for yourself, lessons are available as well. Learn more here.
